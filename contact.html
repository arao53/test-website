Prospective Graduate Students

If you are interested in joining as a PhD student, please apply through the department's official admissions portal and mention WE3 Lab in your statement. You may also email the PI directly with your CV and research interests — we read every inquiry.

Undergraduate & Visiting Researchers

We regularly host undergraduates for independent study and REU projects. Visiting researchers and international students are also welcome — please reach out with a CV and a one-paragraph statement of interest.
